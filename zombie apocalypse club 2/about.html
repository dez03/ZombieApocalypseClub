About Us

We are a highly selective group of dedicated high school students, guided by zombie survivalist/experts, to plan, prepare, and prevail for the inevitable zombie apocalypse. In doing so, we learn many valuable life skills such as and not limited too leadership, teamwork, integrity, hardwork.

We believe that mental and physical toughness summon the courage to engage and develope the heart to survive.

This club was founded on three core principals


1. A zombie is defined as a relentlessly aggressive human, or reanimated human corpse, driven by a biological infection.

2. Enthusiastic debate regarding the historic, scientific, and cultural study of zombies is essential to the survival of the human race.

3. The zombie pandemic is inevitable, and survival of the human race is crucial. It’s simply a matter of when; so be prepared.


The Zombie Apocalypse Club was founded in 2007 as a non-profit dedicated to the historical, cultural, and scientific study of zombies. We've grown to include hundreds of thousands of active members, experts, and volunteers all over the world since then. We decided to share our knowledge with high schools in 2015, and since then, we've been able to collaborate with hundreds of districts across the country.

Our goal is to inform and prepare the youth for very real events that can happen in their life time. We take pride in our work and have many decorated coaches to give our members the very best information from people who have studied zombies for decades. Find out more about our coaches here
