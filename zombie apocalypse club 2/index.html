Our Mission

Zombie Apocalypes Club is a student-led, high school club. Our members are trained to be prepared for the inevitable zombie invasion that is bound to happen. In doing so, we practice a plethora of survival tatics that can help our chances of survival during such event. Our members are at the top of the food chain. Everyone here is a leader. We believe that mental and physical toughness summoning the courage to engage and developing the heart to survive.
