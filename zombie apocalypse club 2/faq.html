Frequently Asked Questions

Please read all the information on this page as it will clarify the sturcture of this club. This page is meant to answer some of the generic questions we get. If you need more information and are still confused, email contact@zombieapocalypseclub.com

When would the club meet?

After school. On our first offical meeting, we will discuss with all the students who show up and determine what days work best for them. We want everyone to show up and to do so consistently. So we schedule around the majority of students.

What if the majority of students can do a certain day of the week that I can't?

Tell the club advisors, and they will schedule a meeting on a day you can go. You must attend at least one meeting a week, every week. Awards will be given to members who show up to every meeting.

What if I can't make it to a meeting?

Email or call your club advisors and tell them at least 24 hours in advance.

What happens if I miss too many meetings?

If attendance is a reoccuring issue for a student. The club advisors will speak to the student individually to see if they can work out a solution. But if nothing works out and absence is still an issue, you will be banished from the club and not be allowed to join any of the for the rest of your life.
